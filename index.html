My Story

I started this brand creating products I loved and would be proud to use myself. Ten years later, I still feel that way. What makes me happiest is hearing how much joy our products bring to people such as a colorful pen to liven up your work desk or a card that is used to bring a note of encouragement to someone who needs it most.
